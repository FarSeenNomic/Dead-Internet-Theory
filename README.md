## Project Description

### Overview
This app is a social media platform designed to help users create and share content.

- Who is it for? A range of users who want to establish themselves with a social media presence. From businesses trying to push their names out, to individuals who want to generate content on the platform and engage in their communities with ease. Or at least that's what our design lead said.
